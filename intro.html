My Biography

 David Batista, a young artist into the art of graphic and media design since, the age of 11. David has been into technology and art for almost his entire life. He loves looking outside the box and sometimes inside of it. Sometimes he looks at things and he gets inspired, he believes that everyone can make art. The first time you start you will not well but you get better, that's how you learn. Imagine a world with no imagination, that would happen if all the artists gave up, but they didn't, never give up on what you work so hard on. He likes to edit and mkaes things from scratch and he likes to take pictures of practically everything, so he never really runs out of pictures, over all there are so many views on the world that you can’t even count them. There’s so many ways to describe a single moment in time, we all make art even if we don’t realize it. Why not be a part of that wonder.
