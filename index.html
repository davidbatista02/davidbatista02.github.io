Biography

I am David Batista a young artist who has gone into the art of graphic design and media design. I started going into design at the age of 11. I’ve been into technology and art for almost my whole life. I love looking outside the box and sometimes I even look inside of it. Sometimes I look at things and I get inspired, there are so many views on the world that you can’t even count them. There’s so many ways to describe a single moment in time, we all make art even if we don’t realize it. .

Graphic Designer

I like to work with with computers
and I like to design logos and just make 
art in general. Graphic Design would take me on a different artistic journey.

Aircraft Engineer

My father is an aircraft engineer and I've 
seen him work and it has always fascinated.
I have some basic knowledge of engineering from my father teaching me.

Photographer/Cinematographer

My mother is a very talented photographer
and the idea of being able to capture a single moment in time has fascinated me.
I love using cameras, i feel free and open to do whatevr i want.

Animator

Me as an artist have explored the magic of digital animation. I've animated small clips and i have been able to transfer my stiil life art into a moving one. This allows me to express myself so much more in different mediums.
