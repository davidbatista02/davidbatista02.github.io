I am David Batista a young artist who has gone into the art of graphic design and media design. I started going into design at the age of 11. I’ve been into technology and art for almost my whole life. I love looking outside the box and sometimes I even look inside of it. Sometimes I look at things and I get inspired, there are so many views on the world that you can’t even count them. There’s so many ways to describe a single moment in time, we all make art even if we don’t realize it.
